## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 🧠 [Neurolift — Cognitive Training App](https://github.com/amdravidranjan/neurolift)
> *React Native · Expo · TypeScript · Vercel*

An all-rounder brain training platform with **15+ gamified exercises** targeting memory, attention, logic, processing speed, and cognitive flexibility. Highlights include:

- **Academy Dashboard** to track your cognitive profile over time
- **Baseline Assessment** for standardized cognitive benchmarking
- **Adaptive Difficulty** — exercises scale automatically to your skill level
- **Offline-First** — train anywhere, no internet required
- **RSVP Speed Reading** — read and process information at up to 1000 wpm
- Deployed on both **Android (EAS)** and **Web (Vercel)**

🌐 **Live Demo**: [neurolift-seven.vercel.app](https://neurolift-seven.vercel.app)

`TypeScript` `React Native` `Expo` `Vercel` `Cognitive Science` `Mobile`

### 🧵 [Sri Balaji Ladies Tailoring — Business Website](https://github.com/amdravidranjan/Women_Tailoring_Shop)
> *HTML · CSS · Vanilla JS · Vercel*

More than a project — this is a tribute to my mother, the sole earning member of our family and the driving force behind her tailoring business in Karur (established 2016). I built this to give her work the visibility it deserves and to help grow her training institute.

Features include:
- **Bilingual UI** — seamless Tamil and English support for the local community
- **30+ Design Showcase** via a touch-responsive Swiper.js carousel
- **Modern UI/UX** — dark theme, glassmorphism cards, magnetic buttons, custom cursor effects
- **WhatsApp & Phone CTA integration** for instant customer contact
- **Embedded Google Maps** for easy navigation to the shop
- **AOS + Vanilla Tilt.js** for scroll animations and 3D card interactions
- Built with **open-source libraries only** — zero paid dependencies

🌐 **Live Site**: [balajiladiestailoring.vercel.app](https://balajiladiestailoring.vercel.app)

`HTML5` `CSS3` `JavaScript` `Swiper.js` `AOS` `Vercel` `Localization`
